Emergency Contacts

Provide at least one emergency contact who will be notified when a welfare check is triggered. Contacts receive automated status updates via SMS or email at three key points: trigger detected, volunteer dispatched, and check complete.

When are contacts notified? (1) When an emergency trigger is detected in the resident's area. (2) When a volunteer has been dispatched. (3) When the welfare check outcome is recorded.
